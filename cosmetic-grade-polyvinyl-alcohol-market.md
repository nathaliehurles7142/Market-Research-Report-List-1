<p><p>The cosmetic grade polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) market is primarily categorized into fully hydrolyzed and partially hydrolyzed types. Fully hydrolyzed PVA has a high degree of hydrolysis, making it suitable for applications requiring excellent adhesive properties and film formation, often used in hair and skin care products. In contrast, partially hydrolyzed PVA retains some ethylene content, offering improved solubility and flexibility, making it ideal for formulations like emulsions and gels, enhancing texture and stability in cosmetic applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD)</li><li>Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food-grade diaphragm pump market includes two primary types: Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) and Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD). AODD pumps utilize compressed air to create pressure and move fluids, offering flexibility and portability, making them ideal for handling a variety of food products. In contrast, EODD pumps employ electric motors for more precise control and consistent flow rates, making them suitable for applications requiring strict hygiene standards and continuous operation in food processing environments.</p></p>
